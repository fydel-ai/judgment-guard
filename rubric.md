# Judgment Guard Rubric
## Evidence strength
High:
- multiple specific supporting facts
- source or concrete basis is visible
- no major unresolved contradiction
Medium:
- partial support
- some assumptions needed
- important unknowns remain
Low:
- mostly generalized reasoning
- little direct support
- strong claims built on weak premises
## Action pressure
High:
- "you should"
- ranked recommendation
- urgency framing
- executive-summary style conclusion
Medium:
- preference implied but not absolute
Low:
- exploratory and comparative
## Overstatement signals
- strong certainty words without support
- elegant causal chain with omitted caveats
- one-sided framing
- alternatives ignored
- recommendation stronger than evidence
